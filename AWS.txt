CREATING AN CLOUD ACCOUNT IN AZURE 

Steps followed :

1. Go to the AWS website.
2. Start free account.
3. Then sign in to the AWS account using your mail id.
4. A sign up page will come up where we need to fill our details.
5. Fill the details , verify your number . Then move to next step.
6. In the next step , we need to give the details of our debit card.
7. Give the details and press okay.
8. After completing all the steps , a 2 rupee will be debited from our account . 
Thus the account has been created.
